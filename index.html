Orange's Turn

    Objective:

    Move all the green kangaroos to the left side of the field and all the orange kangaroos to the right side.
    Ex. 1 of winning combination: Order of green/orange kangaroos does not matter.

    Rules of the game

    1. Each kangaroo can only hop one or two places in an empty grass space. Therefore, kangaroos that are more than 2 spots away are disables and cannot move.
    2.Turns alternate. Orange goes first, then it's green's turn on so on.
    3.You can lose the game with a stalemate. For example, if it's green kangaroo's turn but the empty grass space is more than two spots away from the closest green kanagaroo, then there is a stalemate and you lose the game. Refer to Ex. 4.
    4.Good Luck!
    Ex. 2 of a turn: During orange's turn, kanagaroos can either move one places or two. Kangaroo #3 & #4 can land in the empty green space.
    Ex. 3 of a turn: During green's turn, only one kangaroo is within two spots of the empty grass space, so kangaroo #6 is the only one that can make the hop.
    Ex. 4 of a Stalemate: During green's turn, the empty grass space is more than two spaces away from any green kangaroo. You will lose the game and have to start from the beginning!
